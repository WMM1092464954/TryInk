Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use TryInk, we may collect:

  • Account info: Name and email via Google sign-in
  • Usage data: Design prompts, style preferences, and generation history
  • Photos: Photos you upload for tattoo preview are processed in your browser only and are never sent to our servers
  • Payment info: Processed by PayPal — we do not store your payment details

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate AI tattoo designs based on your prompts
  • Save your design history to your account
  • Track credit usage
  • Improve our service

3. Data Storage

Account data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). Photos uploaded for preview remain in your browser and are never transmitted to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and data storage
  • Cloudflare — Hosting and AI image generation
  • PayPal — Payment processing

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us at support@tryink.art.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@tryink.art
