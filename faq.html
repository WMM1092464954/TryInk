Frequently Asked Questions

How does TryInk work?

Describe the tattoo you want, pick a style, and our AI generates 4 unique designs in seconds. You can then upload a photo of your body and preview exactly how the tattoo will look on your skin.

Is it really free?

Yes! You get 3 free AI design generations. Free previews include a small watermark. For HD watermark-free downloads, you can buy a single download ($2) or a design pack ($5 for 10 generations).

Can I use these designs for an actual tattoo?

Absolutely. All designs come with a commercial use license. We recommend sharing the design with your tattoo artist who can refine it to work perfectly on your skin.

Are my photos stored on your servers?

No. Photos you upload for the body preview are processed entirely in your browser. They never leave your device.

What tattoo styles are available?

We currently support 11 styles: Minimalist, Fine Line, Traditional, Blackwork, Japanese, Watercolor, Dotwork, Realistic, Neo-Traditional, Tribal, and Geometric.

What is the Photo-to-Tattoo converter?

Upload any photo and our edge detection algorithm converts it into a tattoo stencil outline. You can adjust detail level and line thickness, then preview it on your body.

How does the body preview work?

Upload a photo of yourself, select a design, and drag it into position. Use the sliders to adjust size, rotation, and opacity. The "Realistic Blend" mode matches the tattoo to your skin tone and texture.

Can I get a refund?

See our Refund Policy for details. In short: refunds are available for service failures, duplicate charges, and unused credit packs within 7 days.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@tryink.art and we'll get back to you within 48 hours.
